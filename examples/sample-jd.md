# Sample JD: AI Product Manager

## Role

AI Product Manager

## Responsibilities

- Discover user pain points in AI-assisted productivity and workflow automation.
- Define PRDs, MVP scope, success metrics, and product roadmap for LLM-powered features.
- Work with design, engineering, data, and GTM teams to validate AI product experiments.
- Use user feedback, funnel data, and adoption metrics to iterate product direction.

## Requirements

- Product management experience with AI, SaaS, productivity, or workflow tools.
- Strong product sense, user research ability, and structured problem definition.
- Familiarity with LLM applications, prompt/product evaluation, and data-driven iteration.
- Ability to work cross-functionally from discovery to launch.
